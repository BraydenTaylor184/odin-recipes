Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian comfort food known for its rich layers of pasta, savory meat sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta, and melted cheese. It’s a hearty and filling dish, perfect for feeding a crowd or enjoying as leftovers throughout the week.

This recipe features traditional ingredients and layering methods, resulting in a well-balanced, flavorful lasagna with a golden, cheesy top that’s crisped to perfection in the oven.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for cooking

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Boil lasagna noodles according to package directions; drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil and cook onions and garlic until softened.
  4. Add ground beef, cook until browned, then stir in marinara sauce and let simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with egg, salt, and pepper.
  6. In a 9x13 baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce.
  7. Add a layer of noodles, followed by ricotta mixture, then mozzarella.
  8. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with sauce and mozzarella on top.
  9. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese over the top layer.
  10. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes, then uncover and bake for another 15 minutes.
  11. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
